Brawl Stars Game Mode

Gem Grab

Gem Grab maps periodically spawn crystals at the center of the map. The main objective is to collect crystals / gems as a team, and hold at least 10 of these for 15 seconds to win.

Show Down

In Showdown Mode, the players' objective is to be the last Brawler standing! It's every Brawler for himself or herself - use the best of your skills to eliminate all your opponents and come out on top!

Bounty

Bounty Mode will put you in a team of 3, playing against another team of 3. Your objective is to earn the most stars as a team within the time limit to win the game mode!

Heist

In Heist mode, you'll be in a team of 3 Brawlers competing with another team of 3 to destroy the opposing team's safe while keeping your own defended.

Brawl Ball

This game mode is a straight up football game! Two teams of 3 will race to be the first to score 2 goals before the timer runs out!

Big Game

This game mode is a straight up football game! Two teams of 3 will race to be the first to score 2 goals before the timer runs out!

TEAM UP AND JUMP INTO MAYHEM!

Time to BRAWL! Team up with your friends and get ready for epic multiplayer MAYHEM! Brawl Stars is the newest game from the makers of Clash of Clans and Clash Royale. Jump into your favorite game mode and play quick matches with your friends. Shoot 'em up, blow 'em up, punch 'em out and win the BRAWL.
